About Us

PlantTrees is a seed sown by a Vietnam-based team who want to make a genuine difference in the world .

We believe that the biggest changes start small. Amazon, Google, and Apple were all founded in a garage. PlantTrees started in our cozy office surrounded by the buzz and noise of Hanoi .

Most of us are in our early 20s and specialize in web design or content creation. We’ve done a lot with our skills, but we felt like we could have more of an impact on the future of our planet .

Little seeds become big trees

When the idea to make a non-profit search engine to help reforestation was suggested, it rightly sounded like a huge undertaking. Regardless, it wasn’t long before everyone was on board.

We needed to learn a lot in a short time. It meant a lot of meetings, emails, and office karaoke sessions at lunch. Eventually, it all began to take shape.

Since we’ve been working on PlantTress, we’ve seen how quickly the ball can get rolling. It turns out you don’t have to live in Silicon Valley to build a search engine!

Our team of misfits is incredibly proud of what we’ve built. We know you’ll also feel a pang of pride when you see that first tree planted from your account. And then the second, and the third!
